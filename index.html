Problems

No Strategy

The current market for prediction operates without providing users with any strategy support. It's a lot of guesswork.

Inaccurate Price

In the prediction market, an inaccurate price is always a hindrance. There is a huge discrepancy between the price that is locked in and the trading chart used by users in current apps.

Centralized and Single chain

There aren't many apps out there that can support multiple chains. Dependencies and other issues, such as KYC, can arise from centralized apps.

Features

Technical Strategy Based

Strategy-enabled trading view that allows users to make forecasts based on market data rather than guesswork.

Multibet and Claim-free

Users can make multiple bets on each prediction round, as well as future bets based on their strategy. Its Claim-free, makes users focus more on predicting rather than collecting the win amount. Every time a user wins a round, the winnings are immediately transferred to their accounts.

Multi Asset and Multi Chain

It enabled users to predict the price of their favourite cryptocurrencies. Supports multiple blockchains

AI PredictionBot

AI based auto prediction and an option to copy the prediction strategy of other users.
